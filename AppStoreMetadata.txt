Description:
Take control of your supplement routine with Vitamin Shelf — the smart tracker that helps you stay consistent with your vitamins, minerals, and supplements.

Pre-loaded with 20 popular supplements including Vitamin D3, Omega-3, Magnesium, and more. Activate the ones you take, set your schedule, and track every dose throughout the day.

Features:
• 20 pre-loaded popular supplements ready to activate
• Daily dose schedule with one-tap tracking
• Push notification reminders at your scheduled times
• Pill count tracking with low stock alerts
• 28-day calendar view with color-coded adherence
• Weekly adherence charts and streak tracking
• Most missed supplements analysis
• Category breakdown (vitamins, minerals, herbs, amino acids, omega, probiotics)
• Activate, pause, or mute any supplement
• Quick refill to reset pill count
• Add custom supplements with flexible dosing
• Clean dark interface with amber accents
• All data stored locally — no account required
